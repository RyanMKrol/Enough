# T045 — Deck detail screen (tinted hero, sample cards with audio, actions)

## Overview

Build the full-screen deck detail view — accent-tinted hero, progress row, sample-card list
with per-row audio, and the sticky Continue/Practice action bar — and swap it in for T044's
placeholder navigation destination.

## Do

Design reference: .harness/docs/designs/design-spec.md (read-only reference) §5. Services via `\.services`
(`ContentStore`, `AudioService`, `DeckProgressService`, `CardSRSStore`), accent via
`\.accentTheme`.

1. `Sources/Features/DeckDetail/DeckDetailView.swift` — `struct DeckDetailView: View` with
   `init(deckId: String)`. Loads on appear: `DeckInfo` + its `CountryInfo` (for
   `languageName`), first cards via `ContentStore.cards(forDeck:)`, and
   `DeckProgressService.progress(forDeck:)`. Full-screen: `.toolbar(.hidden, for:
   .navigationBar)` and hide the floating tab bar (it only exists on top-level tabs).
   Layout top→bottom:
   - **Hero** (background `accentTheme.tint`, bottom corners square, generous top padding for
     the status bar): top row = two 36pt frosted circular buttons (`.ultraThinMaterial`
     circles) — left `chevron.backward` → `dismiss()`; right `ellipsis` → `Menu` with one
     destructive item "Reset deck progress" that shows a `confirmationDialog`
     ("Reset deck progress?" / destructive "Reset" / "Cancel") and on confirm deletes the
     deck's rows via `CardSRSStore.records(forDeck:)` + context delete, then reloads.
     Below: 56pt RoundedRectangle(r14) white tile with the deck's SF Symbol `icon` in
     `accentTheme.accent`; deck `title` 30 Bold; subtitle
     "\(country.languageName) · \(deck.subtitle)" 15 Regular `accentTheme.deep`; HStack of
     `MetaChip`s: "\(cardCount) cards", "~\(minutes) min", "Audio" — minutes = cardCount × 25s
     rounded UP to whole minutes.
   - **Progress row** (on canvas `#f2f2f7`, padding 22): "\(learned) of \(total) learned"
     15 Regular `#6b6b70` + Spacer + "\(pct)%" 15 Semibold (pct = learned/total rounded to
     whole %; "8 of 30 learned" → "27%"); under it `AnimatedProgressBar(progress:,
     tint: accentTheme.accent)`.
   - **Samples**: `EyebrowLabel("In this deck")`, then a white card (radius 20) listing the
     FIRST 5 cards, hairline dividers: each row (vertical padding 12) = VStack(leading,
     spacing 3): `target` 17 Semibold; "\(pronunciation) · \(english)" 15 Regular `#6b6b70`,
     lineLimit 1; trailing `PulsingAudioButton(size: 34, isPulsing: false)` whose action calls
     `services.audio.play(fileName: card.audio)` (the button's built-in ripple-once fires on
     tap).
   - **Sticky bottom bar** overlaid at the bottom over a white gradient fade
     (LinearGradient white.opacity(0)→white, ~110pt): VStack(spacing 10):
     `Button("Continue learning")` styled `PrimaryButtonStyle(background:
     accentTheme.accent)` with a SUBTLE shadow (`0 4 10 accent@20%` — a second button sits
     directly beneath, design-spec §1.3) — `// TODO(T047): launch learn session`; and
     `Button("Practice all \(cardCount)")` styled `TintedButtonStyle` —
     `// TODO(T047): launch practice session`.
2. Replace the placeholder destination: in `Sources/Features/Home/HomeView.swift`'s
   `.navigationDestination(for: HomeDestination.self)`, return
   `DeckDetailView(deckId: deckId)` instead of the T044 `Text` placeholder (Home scope is
   included for exactly this edit).
3. `#Preview` with a real fixture deck id ("jp-greetings") on the preview services.

Copy strings EXACT: "In this deck", "Continue learning", "Practice all \(n)",
"Reset deck progress".

## Done when

- Tapping a deck row on Home pushes `DeckDetailView` (placeholder gone); back chevron
  dismisses; the tab bar is not visible on this screen.
- Hero shows icon tile, title, "Japanese · <subtitle>" line and the three MetaChips; progress
  row shows "N of M learned" + percent + animated accent bar consistent with
  `DeckProgressService`.
- Exactly 5 sample rows; tapping a row's speaker plays that card's clip via
  `AudioService.play(fileName:)` and ripples once.
- Ellipsis → "Reset deck progress" → confirm wipes the deck's SRS rows and the progress row
  drops to "0 of M learned".
- Both action buttons render (inert TODOs for T047); the primary's shadow is visibly subtler
  than the standard accent-button shadow.
- Verify on the simulator (`./build_run.sh`, `-demo-state` once T042 exists): screenshot of a
  deck detail shows the tinted hero + chips, progress row, "IN THIS DECK" samples with
  speaker buttons, and the two stacked bottom actions — matching design-spec §5.
