## Overview

🔒 needs-human. Assemble and submit the App Store Connect package. Everything here is
ASC-side or judgment work: metadata, screenshots, ratings, labels. The technical gates
(privacy manifest T066, policy T071, icon T063, IAP products T061, acceptance T060) are done
by the time this unblocks.

## Do

1. ASC metadata: name "Enough", subtitle (suggest: "Just enough of a language"), description,
   keywords, support URL, copyright, contact info.
2. Privacy: enter the privacy policy URL (from T071); fill the App Privacy questionnaire to
   match the manifest — no data collected. Nutrition labels must agree with
   `PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`.
3. Age rating questionnaire (current 5-tier system): everything "None" → 4+.
4. Screenshots: capture required sizes (6.9" and 6.5" iPhone at minimum) — the `-demo-state`
   launch argument gives a populated, design-matching app for capture. Optionally run the
   Axiom `screenshot-validator` agent over the final set (placeholder text, wrong dimensions,
   debug indicators).
5. IAP: verify every product has its review screenshot uploaded and ALL products are attached
   to the app version (App Version → In-App Purchases) — both are silent Guideline 2.1
   rejections.
6. Export compliance: `ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption` false is already in the Info.plist —
   confirm ASC reflects it.
7. Build & upload an archive (Xcode Organizer or `xcodebuild archive` + `-exportArchive`),
   submit for review.

## Done when

The build is submitted for App Review with all metadata/IAP/rating/privacy sections complete,
and the owner marks this done via `.harness/scripts/mark-done.sh T074`.
