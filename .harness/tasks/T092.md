## Overview

The owner has decided Enough is a brand-new app for new phones only, so it ships iOS 26-only — which unblocks adopting iOS 26-only APIs (e.g. Liquid Glass glassEffect()) unconditionally, with no #available guards or pre-26 fallbacks.

## Do
