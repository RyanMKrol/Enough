## Overview

The Home "Continue" hero card (`Sources/Features/Home/ContinueCard.swift`) has two small fidelity
gaps against the design mock: its progress ring does not show the percent label the design puts
inside the ring, and its detail/meta line wraps onto 2-3 lines instead of sitting on one line.

## Do

In `Sources/Features/Home/ContinueCard.swift` only:

1. **Show the ring percent.** The `ProgressRing(...)` call (around line 14-20) currently passes
   `showsPercent: false`. The shared `ProgressRing` design-system component already renders a
   centered `"NN%"` label on its own when `showsPercent` is true. Change this call site to
   `showsPercent: true` so the percent shows inside the ring, matching the design's Continue card
   (e.g. the mock shows "40%" inside the ring). Do not modify the `ProgressRing` component — only
   this call site here in `ContinueCard.swift`.

2. **Keep the meta line on one line.** The `detailLine` `Text` (around line 29-31, e.g.
   "8 of 30 cards · about 6 min left") wraps because the middle text column loses width to the
   trailing `Spacer()`. Make that line render on a SINGLE line at the default text size on standard
   iPhone widths — for example give the middle text `VStack` `.layoutPriority(1)` so it wins the
   width it needs over the `Spacer`, and add `.lineLimit(1)` with a modest `.minimumScaleFactor`
   (about 0.85) on the detail `Text` as a safety net so it never wraps. A slightly smaller ring
   footprint is acceptable if it helps, but is not required.

Do not change the card's gradient, corner radius (24), the 44pt play button, the accent theming, or
any copy strings. This is layout/appearance polish only, no behaviour change.

## Done when

- `ContinueCard`'s `ProgressRing` is constructed with `showsPercent: true`, so the ring shows its
  percent label.
- At the default text size on an iPhone 17 Pro simulator, the Continue card's detail line
  ("N of M cards · about K min left") renders on ONE line with no wrapping, matching the design's
  Continue card.
- The card's gradient background, corner radius, play button, accent tint, and all copy are
  unchanged from before.
- A `./build_run.sh` screenshot (launched with `-demo-state` so the card shows real progress)
  confirms, at default text size, that the ring displays a percent AND the meta line is a single
  line. Record what you observed in the worklog.
