# T009 — Welcome card-stack hero (premium panel)

## Overview

Build the welcome screen's hero: three fanned flashcards that gently bob, the front one showing
the chosen "premium panel" composition (Japan header band, ありがとう, romaji pill). Static
content is intentional — it appears only on the Welcome screen. Exact design in
design-spec.md §2.1.

## Do

1. Create `Sources/DesignSystem/CardStackHero.swift` with exactly this view:
   - `struct CardStackHero: View` with `init()`. Fixed stage frame **255 × 292**.
2. Compose three cards, each **165 × 224**, `RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 28)`:
   - Back-left: fill `#e8f5ff` (use `Color(hex: 0xe8f5ff)`), `rotationEffect(.degrees(-10))`,
     offset x −36, y 6.
   - Back-right: fill `#fff0df`, `rotationEffect(.degrees(10))`, offset x 36, y 6.
   - Front (on top, upright, centered): white fill, 1pt border `Color.black.opacity(0.07)`,
     shadow `Color.black.opacity(0.14)`, radius 21, y 20. Content, clipped to the card shape:
     - Header band: full-width, background `#fff0ee`, padding 16 vertical / 18 horizontal is too
       tall for a 224pt card — use padding 12 vertical / 14 horizontal — containing an
       `HStack(spacing: 8)`: a 30×30 white `RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 9)` flag chip holding
       a centered 13pt `Circle` filled `#e24947`, then `Text("Japan")` 13pt semibold `#a43b38`.
       A 1pt bottom border line in `#fedbd7`.
     - Stage below the band (fills the rest, content centered, spacing 14): `Text("ありがとう")`
       24pt bold `EnoughColor.label`; below it a pronunciation pill: `Text("arigatō")` 12.5pt
       medium, color `#a45953`, kerning `12.5 * 0.04`, background `#ffe9e6`, padding
       6 vertical / 12 horizontal, `Capsule` shape.
3. Bob animation: all three cards oscillate vertically, `.easeInOut(duration:
   Motion.bobDuration)` (4s) repeating forever with autoreverse — front ±6pt, backs ±4pt, with
   phase offsets (delay 0 / 0.5s / 1.0s) so they don't move in lockstep.
4. Add a `#Preview` rendering `CardStackHero()` on a white background.

Conventions: single app target, SwiftUI only, 2-space indent, SwiftLint strict (≤130 cols) +
swift-format strict. The hero is self-contained and takes no parameters in v1 — do NOT wire it
to the content catalog. Touch only `Sources/DesignSystem/**` (and `Tests/**` if adding optional
tests).

## Done when

- `Sources/DesignSystem/CardStackHero.swift` compiles with the exact name and a parameterless
  `init()` (the Welcome screen task embeds `CardStackHero()` verbatim).
- The three-card fan matches the pinned geometry (165×224 r28 cards; backs `#e8f5ff` at −10° and
  `#fff0df` at +10°; front white with the 0.07-alpha border and deep 0.14-alpha shadow) inside
  the 255×292 stage.
- The front card shows the premium-panel composition: `#fff0ee` band with `#fedbd7` bottom
  border, 30×30 flag chip with `#e24947` dot, "Japan" in `#a43b38`, centered ありがとう 24 bold,
  and the "arigatō" pill (`#a45953` on `#ffe9e6`).
- All three cards bob on a 4s ease-in-out loop (front ±6pt, backs ±4pt, phases offset).
- The `#Preview` block exists.
