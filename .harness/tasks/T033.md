## Overview

Build the "Where are you headed?" country-picker step (Step 1 of 3) with selectable country
cards from the catalog, a dashed "More soon" tile, and the Continue button that morphs graphite
→ the selected country's accent — the "app becomes yours" moment.

## Do

1. Read design-spec.md §2.2 and architecture.md. Countries come from
   `services.contentStore.catalog()` — NEVER hardcode the country list.
2. Create `Sources/Features/Onboarding/CountryCardView.swift` — pure presentational
   `struct CountryCardView: View { init(country: CountryInfo, isSelected: Bool,
   action: () -> Void) }`:
   - White card, radius 20, interior padding ~16, full-width column: 46pt flag tile (the
     country's `flagEmoji` at ~28pt centered on a rounded-square (radius 12) of that country's
     `AccentTheme` tint color), name 18 semibold `#1c1c1e`, subtitle
     "<nativeLanguageName> · <decks.count> decks" 13 regular `#6b6b70` (e.g. "日本語 · 4 decks").
   - Unselected: `overlay` stroke `Color.black.opacity(0.08)` 1pt, no shadow.
   - Selected: stroke of the COUNTRY's accent 2.5pt, shadow
     `color: accent.opacity(0.25), radius: 14, y: 6`, plus a 22pt filled accent circle with a
     white `checkmark` (11pt bold) pinned top-right (offset ~8 outside the padding), and a
     light impact haptic on selection.
   - Selection state animates with a spring (`.spring(duration: 0.3)`).
3. Create `Sources/Features/Onboarding/CountryPickerView.swift` —
   `struct CountryPickerView: View`, reading `\.services` and the shared `OnboardingDraft`
   from the environment:
   - Toolbar: the shared `OnboardingBackButton` (T031).
   - Header block: `EyebrowLabel("STEP 1 OF 3")`, title "Where are you headed?" (30–32 bold,
     tracking −0.02em), subtitle "Pick a country to unlock its decks." (15 regular `#6b6b70`).
   - `LazyVGrid` with 2 flexible columns (spacing 12): one `CountryCardView` per catalog
     country; tap calls `draft.selectCountry(country)` (sets id + resolved CountryInfo; the
     flow's accent environment re-tints immediately per T031).
   - Last grid cell: "More soon" tile — dashed border
     (`strokeBorder(style: StrokeStyle(lineWidth: 1.5, dash: [6]))`, `Color.black.opacity(0.15)`),
     radius 20, containing "More soon" 15 semibold `#8e8e93` over `catalog.comingSoon`
     joined with " · " ("Italy · Spain · Thailand") 13 regular `#a0a0a6`. Non-interactive.
   - Screen background `EnoughColor.surface` (white); content scrolls if needed; horizontal
     padding ~22.
   - Pinned bottom: `Button("Continue")` with
     `PrimaryButtonStyle(background: draft.selectedCountryId == nil ?
     EnoughColor.graphite : draft.accent.accent)` — i.e. graphite until a selection exists,
     then the selected country's accent; wrap the color change in
     `.animation(.easeInOut(duration: 0.35), ...)` so it MORPHS rather than snaps. Disabled
     (`.disabled` + 50% opacity) while no country is selected. Action: push `.tripShape`.
4. Replace the `.country` placeholder destination in `OnboardingFlowView` with
   `CountryPickerView`.
5. `#Preview` (preview services; nothing selected + Japan selected variants).

## Done when

- The grid renders exactly the catalog's countries (3 in the fixture) + the dashed "More soon"
  tile; tapping a card shows the selected treatment (accent ring, tinted shadow, check badge)
  and updates `OnboardingDraft`.
- Continue is disabled until a selection exists, then morphs graphite→accent (animated) and
  pushes the trip-shape step; going back and returning shows the selection intact.
- Selecting France re-tints the flow's accent environment (visible on the card ring + button)
  to `#3d7ede` without restart.
- Verify on the simulator (`./build_run.sh` then navigate: Get started): screenshot of this
  step shows the eyebrow "STEP 1 OF 3", the title/subtitle copy exactly, a 2×2 grid (Japan,
  France, Germany, More soon), and the graphite Continue; if a scripted tap to select Japan is
  feasible, capture the selected state too — otherwise state in the worklog that the selected
  state was verified via preview.
