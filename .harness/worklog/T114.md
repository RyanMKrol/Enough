## What I did
Fixed the review-flow blank-screen bug — the review twin of T112's learn-session fix.

1. `ReviewSessionView.swift`: replaced the `onAppear` attached to the `else`-branch
   placeholder (which mutated `viewModel` from a branch about to be swapped out) with a
   `.task` on the stable `NavigationStack` root, mirroring `MCSessionView.swift`'s fix.
   `viewModel` is now reliably constructed once the view appears.
2. `ReviewsTabView.swift`: replaced the optional `@State reviewSessionEngine` +
   `isShowingReviewSession` boolean pair with a single `@State reviewSession:
   SessionEnginePresentation?` (reusing the `Identifiable` wrapper T112 introduced in
   `MCSessionView.swift`), and switched `.fullScreenCover(isPresented:)` to
   `.fullScreenCover(item: $reviewSession)`. `startReviewSession()` now sets
   `reviewSession = SessionEnginePresentation(...)` directly instead of setting an
   optional engine and a separate presenting flag. Both entry paths ("Start review"
   button and the `consumePendingActionIfNeeded()` App Intent auto-start) already funnel
   through `startReviewSession()`, so both are covered.
