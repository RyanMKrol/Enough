# T033 — Country picker screen (Step 1) with graphite→accent morph

## 2026-07-16 Build

### What was done

Created `Sources/Features/Onboarding/CountryCardView.swift`:
- Pure presentational card: `init(country: CountryInfo, isSelected: Bool, action: () -> Void)`.
- White card, radius 20, 46pt flag tile (28pt emoji on a radius-12 square tinted with the
  country's `AccentTheme.tint`), name 18 semibold `#1c1c1e`, subtitle
  "<nativeLanguageName> · <decks.count> decks" 13 regular `#6b6b70`.
- Unselected: 1pt `Color.black.opacity(0.08)` stroke, no shadow.
- Selected: 2.5pt accent stroke, `accent.opacity(0.25)` shadow (radius 14, y 6), 22pt filled
  accent circle + white bold 11pt checkmark top-right (offset 8 outside padding), light
  impact haptic on selection, `.spring(duration: 0.3)` animation.

Created `Sources/Features/Onboarding/CountryPickerView.swift`:
- Reads `\.services` and the shared `OnboardingDraft` (`@Environment(OnboardingDraft.self)`)
  from the environment.
- Toolbar: shared `OnboardingBackButton`. Header: `EyebrowLabel("STEP 1 OF 3")`, title
  "Where are you headed?" (30 bold, tracking −0.02em), subtitle "Pick a country to unlock its
  decks." (15 regular `#6b6b70`).
- `LazyVGrid`, 2 flexible columns, spacing 12: one `CountryCardView` per
  `services.contentStore.catalog().countries` (loaded via `.onAppear`, never hardcoded), plus a
  dashed "More soon" tile (`strokeBorder` dash [6], `Color.black.opacity(0.15)`) showing
  `catalog.comingSoon.joined(separator: " · ")`.
- Pinned-bottom `Continue` button (`safeAreaInset(edge: .bottom)`) using
  `PrimaryButtonStyle(background: draft.selectedCountryId == nil ? EnoughColor.graphite :
  draft.accent.accent)`, wrapped in `.animation(.easeInOut(duration: 0.35), value:
  draft.selectedCountryId)` so the color morphs; disabled + 50% opacity until a selection
  exists. Action invokes the injected `onContinue` closure.
- Two `#Preview`s: no selection, and Japan pre-selected.

Wired `OnboardingFlowView`'s `.country` destination to
`CountryPickerView { path.append(.tripShape) }`, replacing the `Text("Country")` placeholder.

- Fresh launch → tapped "Get started" → landed on the country picker. Screenshot confirmed:
  eyebrow "STEP 1 OF 3", exact title "Where are you headed?" / subtitle "Pick a country to
  unlock its decks.", a grid showing Japan/France/Germany (the 3-country fixture) + the dashed
  "More soon · Italy · Spain · Thailand" tile, and a graphite, disabled Continue button.
- Tapped the Japan card: it gained the 2.5pt red accent ring, tinted shadow, and a filled red
  checkmark badge; Continue animated from graphite to Japan red (`#e24947`).
- Tapped the France card: the WHOLE flow's accent environment re-tinted live — Japan's ring
  cleared, France gained the ring/badge, and Continue morphed to `#3d7ede` (no restart needed).
- Tapped Continue with France selected: pushed to the `.tripShape` placeholder screen
  ("Trip shape" stub, owned by a later task), confirming the push action fires correctly.
- Did not re-verify "going back preserves selection" via a live back-tap, because the
  `.tripShape` destination is still the unbuilt placeholder `Text` (no back button — that's
  out of this task's scope). This is architecturally guaranteed instead: `OnboardingDraft` is a
  single `@State`-owned `@Observable` instance injected once via `.environment(draft)` at the
  `OnboardingFlowView` level (see T031), so popping the nav path back to `.country` re-renders
  the same instance/selection rather than resetting it — the same mechanism already exercised
  by T031/T032's tests.
- Simulator quirk observed (unrelated to this task's code): with two simulators booted, `axe`'s
  `--label` tap resolution used a stale/mismatched coordinate space and missed real controls;
  raw `-x/-y` coordinates derived from actual screenshots landed correctly and were used for
  all interaction steps above.
